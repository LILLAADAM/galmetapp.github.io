🎡 How to Play

1. Enter your bet amount (minimum 10 coins).
2. Click SPIN and watch the wheel turn.
3. The pointer decides your multiplier (0x to 10x).
4. Win = bet × multiplier (if multiplier > 0).
5. After each spin, wait 3 seconds before next spin.
6. Top up coins when needed via the TOP UP button.

🎲 The wheel is fair and uses a certified random system. Good luck!
