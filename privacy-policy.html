Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 12, 2024  ·  Galmet

Galmet ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use the Galmet app, how we use and safeguard it, and the choices available to you.
Section 01

Information We Collect

Personal Information Full name, email address, phone number, date of birth, profile photo, and other details you voluntarily provide when creating or updating your account.
Usage Information Data about how you interact with app features, your in-app preferences, and account settings that help us understand how the service is used.
Device Information Device model, operating system version, app version, IP address, and unique device identifiers required for core functionality and security.
Location Information Approximate or precise location data, collected only when you have explicitly granted permission through your device settings.
Section 02

How We Use Your Information

To provide, operate, and maintain the Galmet service reliably.
To personalize your in-app experience based on your preferences and usage patterns.
To research, develop, and improve existing and new product features.
To send transactional messages, service updates, and customer support communications.
To detect, prevent, and address fraudulent activity, abuse, and security vulnerabilities.
To fulfill applicable legal obligations and respond to lawful requests from authorities.
Section 03

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only in the following circumstances:

Service Providers Trusted third-party vendors who assist with hosting, analytics, customer support, and payment processing, under strict confidentiality obligations.
Business Partners Partners who collaborate with us to deliver specific features you choose to use, limited to what is necessary for that purpose.
Legal Authorities When required by applicable law, a court order, or to protect the rights, property, and safety of Galmet and its users.
Section 04

Your Choices & Rights

Access & Update You may review or correct your account information at any time through the in-app settings menu.
Delete Account You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data by emailing us at galmetteam@gmail.com. We will process requests in accordance with applicable law.
Marketing Opt-Out You may unsubscribe from promotional emails at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link included in each message.
Section 05

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. While we strive for high security standards, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is entirely risk-free.

Section 06

Children's Privacy

Galmet is designed for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has submitted personal data through our app, please contact us immediately and we will take prompt corrective action.

Section 07

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. In all such cases, we apply appropriate safeguards consistent with applicable data protection laws to ensure your information remains protected.
